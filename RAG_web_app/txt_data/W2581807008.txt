TITLE: Terrestrial discharges mediate trophic shifts and enhance methylmercury accumulation in estuarine biota
AUTHOR: ['Sofi Jonsson', 'Agneta Andersson', 'Mats Nilsson', 'Ulf Skyllberg', 'Erik Lundberg', 'Jeffra K. Schaefer', 'Staffan Åkerblom', 'Erik Björn']
ABSTRACT:
Terrestrial discharge can cause pelagic zone trophic shifts and enhance methylmercury accumulation in plankton three- to sixfold.
