TITLE: Heterotrophic dinoflagellates: a significant component of microzooplankton biomass and major grazers of diatoms in the sea
AUTHOR: ['EB Sherr', 'BF Sherr']
ABSTRACT:
An association between diatom blooms and heterotrophic dinoflagellates has been noted in many environments.Feeding rate measurements have indicated that heterotrophic dinoflagellates may be significant consumers of diatom blooms.
