TITLE: Coral reef islands can accrete vertically in response to sea level rise
AUTHOR: ['Gerd Masselink', 'Edward Beetham', 'Paul S. Kench']
ABSTRACT:
Coral reef islands are morphodynamically resilient landforms that can adjust to sea level rise through vertical accretion.
