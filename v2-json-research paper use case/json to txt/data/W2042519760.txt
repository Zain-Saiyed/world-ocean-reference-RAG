TITLE: Criteria for temperature alerts in cod supply chains
AUTHOR: ['Tómas Hafliðason', 'Guðrún Ólafsdóttir', 'Sigurður G. Bogason', 'Gunnar Stefánsson']
ABSTRACT:
Purpose Wireless sensor network (WSN) technologies are now available to implement real time temperature monitoring systems in food supply chains. The aim of this paper is to examine different types of methods and criteria to establish alerts in decision support systems in perishable food supply chains.
