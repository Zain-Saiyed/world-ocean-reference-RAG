TITLE: High seas fisheries play a negligible role in addressing global food security
AUTHOR: ['Laurenne Schiller', 'Megan Bailey', 'Jennifer Jacquet', 'Enric Sala']
ABSTRACT:
On the basis of the available data, high seas fisheries do not make a direct or crucial contribution to global food security.
